Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to FM-200 Calculator ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Project information (room dimensions, specifications)
  • Contact information (if provided for feedback)
  • Calculation preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Visitor count statistics
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our calculator services
  • Improving user experience and functionality
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our tools
  • Saving your calculation preferences locally
  • Responding to feedback and inquiries

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your calculation data and preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Visitor Counter: We maintain a visitor count using localStorage to track total visits. This is anonymous and doesn't identify individual users.

No Server Storage: We do not store your personal information or calculation data on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography
  • Font Awesome: For icons
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and localStorage for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Saving calculation data locally
  • Tracking visitor count
  • Maintaining user preferences

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your localStorage data through browser settings
  • Disable JavaScript (though this will affect functionality)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
